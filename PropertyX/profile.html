About PropertX

PropertX is a private property development company registered in 2025, focused on designing, building, and managing Purpose-Built Student Accommodation (PBSA) in underserved towns of the Eastern Cape, South Africa.

Our Goal

To provide safe, modern, and affordable student accommodation in areas with high demand and low supply, such as Mthatha, Butterworth, and Queenstown. We aim to support the academic journey of university students by offering housing that meets their needs and government standards.
